About Me

I am a student studying game design at the Rochester Institute of Technology (graduating Spring 2027) specializing in 3D asset production and systems design. My 3D work covers the full production pipeline, concepting, modeling, and texturing game-ready assets using Maya, ZBrush, and Substance Painter with a particular interest in crafting expansive worlds and environments. In addition to 3D design, I build gameplay mechanics and tools using C++, C#, and Unreal's Blueprints with a strong foundation in object-oriented programming. I'm actively looking to contribute to game projects where strong art and gameplay systems work together to create memorable player experiences.
